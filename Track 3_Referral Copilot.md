# Referral Copilot

## Problem Statement

**Question:** Where should a patient or coordinator actually go?

Healthcare directories often contain incomplete, outdated, or unverified facility information. Patients and coordinators need a faster way to identify nearby facilities that appear capable of delivering a specific type of care, along with transparent evidence supporting those recommendations.

This project builds an **evidence-aware referral assistant** that helps users search for healthcare facilities by care need and location.

---

## Example Queries

- Dialysis near Jaipur  
- Emergency surgery near Patna  
- NICU in Kolkata  
- Cancer treatment near Mumbai
